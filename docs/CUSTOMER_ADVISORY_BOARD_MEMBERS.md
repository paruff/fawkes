## How to Join

We're actively recruiting our inaugural Customer Advisory Board members!

### Qualifications

- Active Fawkes user (3+ months)
- Running Fawkes in production or staging
- Platform engineering or DevOps leadership role
- Available for quarterly meetings (4-6 hours per quarter)

### Apply

Interested in joining? We'd love to hear from you!

1. Review the [Customer Advisory Board Charter](CUSTOMER_ADVISORY_BOARD.md)
2. Complete the [Nomination Template](research/templates/cab-nomination.md)
3. Submit via GitHub Discussion or email to product-team@fawkes.local
