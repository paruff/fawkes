## Personas

The platform serves multiple user types:

1. **Platform Engineers** - Build and maintain the platform
2. **Application Developers** - Use platform services
3. **SREs** - Operate and monitor systems
4. **Engineering Leaders** - Make decisions based on metrics
5. **Platform Learners** - Learning platform engineering through Dojo
