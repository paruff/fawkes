# Fawkes Roadmap

This file tracks planned and aspirational features for Fawkes. Items marked
**[aspirational]** are goals, not committed deliverables — they depend on community
growth, funding, and contributor availability.

---

## Current Release: v0.3.0 (December 2025)

- ✅ Core architecture and governance
- ✅ Dojo learning system design
- ✅ Security plane — SBOM, image signing, policy enforcement
- ✅ DORA metrics automation and dashboards
- ✅ Multi-cloud support (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- ✅ GitOps workflows with ArgoCD
- ✅ Observability stack (Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry)
- ✅ Jenkins CI/CD with golden path templates
- ✅ Backstage developer portal
- ✅ Mattermost collaboration platform
- ✅ Extensions — AI (Weaviate, RAG service) and Data Platform (DataHub) as opt-in add-ons
